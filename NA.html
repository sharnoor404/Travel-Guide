Antelope Canyon

Antelope Canyon is the most photographed Canyon in the world. It includes two separate, scenic slot canyon sections, referred to as Upper Antelope Canyon, and Lower Antelope Canyon.

Location: Arizona

Floor Elevation: 3,704 ft

Best time to visit: Between the end of March and early October

Other places to visit nearby: Horseshoe Bend, Navajo Village Heritage Center, Glen Canyon Dam Overlook and many more.

Yellowstone National Park

It is a wilderness recreation area atop a volcanic hot spot.Yellowstone features dramatic canyons, alpine rivers, lush forests, hot springs and gushing geysers, including its most famous, Old Faithful.It's also home to hundreds of animal species.

Location: Idaho, Montana, and Wyoming, USA

Established: 1 March 1872

Best time to visit: From April to May and between September and October

Other places to visit nearby: Grand Tetons National Park, Virginia City, Western town of Cody and many more.

Niagara Falls

Niagara Falls,is a group of three waterfalls at the southern end of Niagara Gorge .Its site on the Niagara River's western shore overlooks the Horseshoe Falls, the cascades' most expansive section.

Location: USA

Elevation: 99 m

Best time to visit: June to August

Other places to visit nearby: Horseshoe Falls, Cave of the winds and many more.

Chichen Itza

Chichen Itza is an archaeological site in Yucatan and one of the most visited locations in Mexico. Rich in history and the centre of pilgrimage for the ancient Maya civilization for over 1,000 years.

Location: Yucatan, Mexico

Best time to visit: November through March

Other islands to visit nearby: El Castillo, Ik Kil, Sacred Cenote and many more.

Disneyland

It is the first of two theme parks built at the Disneyland Resort and the only theme park designed and built to completion under the direct supervision of Walt Disney.

Location: Anaheim, California

Opened: 17 July 1955

Things to do near Disneyland: Go See a Show at House of Blues Anaheim, Watch a Baseball Game at Angel Stadium and many more.
