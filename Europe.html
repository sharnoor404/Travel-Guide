Eiffel Tower

The capital of France is a global center known for its art, culture, fashion and gastronomy.The Eiffel Tower here is a wrought-iron lattice tower, which has been named after the engineer whose company designed and built it.

Location: Champ de Mars, France

Height: 324 m(to tip)

Construction Started: 28 January 1887

Other places to visit nearby: The Musée Rodin, The Parc des Expositions de la Porte de Versailles, The Palais de Tokyo and many more.

Cathedral Of Santa Maria Del Fiore

The cathedral of Florence, was begun in 1296 in the Gothic style to a design of Arnolfo di Cambio and was structurally completed by 1436.The 3 building of the cathedral complex are part of the UNESCO World Heritage Site

Location: Piazza del Duomo, Italy

Construction Started: 9 September 1296

Height: 153 m

Other places to visit nearby: Giotto's Bell Tower, Baptistery, Santo Spirito Church and many more.

Burano Island

The island is known for its brightly colored fishermen's houses and its casual eateries serving seafood from the lagoon.Other attractions of the island are its lace shops and local butter cookies.

Location: Metropolitan City of Venice, Italy

Population: 2,777

How to get here?: A vaporetto line runs from Venice to Burano

Other places to visit nearby: Lace Museum, San Martino, La Perla Gallery and many more.

Plaza de Callao

Located in the center of the capital of Spain, the square remembers its name from the May 1866 battle of Callao between the Spanish naval forces and Peruvian army.The eternal bustle and art deco style makes this place the heart of Madrid.

Location: Madrid, Spain

Other places to visit nearby: Plaza Mayor, Carrion Building , Monasterio de las Descalzas Reales and many more.

Stockholm City Hall

The Stockholm City Hall is the building of Municipal Council for the City of Stockholm in Sweden.It houses offices and conference rooms as well as ceremonial halls, and the luxury restaurant Stadshuskällaren

Location: Stockholm, Sweden

Construction started: 1911

Height: 106 m

Other places to visit nearby: Gamla stan, Vasa Museum, The Royal Palace, Skansen and many more.

Seville

The capital of southern Spain’s Andalusia region Seville is famous for flamenco dancing. Major landmarks include the ornate Alcázar castle complex, Plaza de Toros de la Maestranza bullring. The Gothic Seville Cathedral is the site of Christopher Columbus’s tomb.

Location: Seville, Spain

Best time to visit: March to May

Other places to visit nearby: Royal Alcázar of Seville, Plaza de España, Catedral de Sevilla and many more.
