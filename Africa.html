Cape Town

Also known as the Mother City, Cape town is port city on South Africa’s southwest coast,beneath the imposing Table Mountain with slowly rotating cable cars and Robben Island, a museum which back then held Nelson Mandela as a prisoner.

Location: Cape Town, South Africa

Best time to visit: March to May and from September to November. These shoulder seasons boast enviable weather, fewer crowds, and lower prices.

Places to visit in Cape Town: Clifton and Camps Bay Beaches, Table Mountain, Robben Island, The Victoria and Alfred Waterfront and many more.

Tanzania

An East African country known for its vast wilderness areas. They include the plains of Serengeti National Park, a safari mecca populated by the “big five” game (elephant, lion, leopard, buffalo, rhino), and Kilimanjaro National Park, home to Africa’s highest mountain. Offshore lie the tropical islands of Zanzibar, with Arabic influences, and Mafia, with a marine park home to whale sharks and coral reefs.

Location: Tanzania, East Africa

Accepted Currency Tanzanian shilling

Official Language Swahili, English

Best time to visit: Dry season, from late June to October,

Places to visit in Tanzania: Ngorongoro Conservation Area, Serengeti National Park, Arusha, Dar es Salaam, Stone Town,Zanzibar Archipelago and many more.

Mehrangarh Fort

Mehrangarh Fort stands a hundred feet in splendor on a perpendicular cliff, four hundred feet above the sky line of Jodhpur. Burnished red sand stone, imposing, invincible and yet with a strange haunting beauty that beckons . Much has been written about the Citadel of the Sun, for truly, it is one of the most impressive in all Rajasthan. So colossal are its proportions that Rudyard Kipling called it “ the work of giants”.

Location: Jodhpur,India

Built in: 1459 by Rao Jodha

Best time to visit: Mid October to Mid March

Other places to visit nearby: Rao Jodha Desert Rock Park,Umaid Bhawan Palace Museum, Mandore Garden,Aswant Thada, and many more.

Boracay

The island comprises the barangays of Manoc-Manoc, Balabag, and Yapak in the municipality of Malay, in Aklan Province. Apart from its white sand beaches, Boracay is also famous for being one of the world's top destinations for relaxation.It has been awarded as the best island of the world in the years 2012 and 2014.

Location: Boracay, Philippines

Area: 0.32 km²

Best time to visit: November to April

Other islands to visit nearby: Crystal Cove Island, Crocodile Island, Panay Island, Carabao Island, Magic Island.

Segara Anak Lake

Segara Anak is a crater lake in the caldera that formed during the explosive volcanic eruption of Mount Samalas in 1257. The mystifyingly blue colour of the lake gives Segara Anak its name: Small Ocean.

Location: Lombok Island, Indonesia

Area: 11.3 km²

Other places to visit in Indonesia: Gili Islands, Mount Bromo, Beaches of Bali, Lake Toba and many more.

Temple of Heaven

The Temple of Heaven is an imperial complex of religious buildings situated in the southeastern part of central Beijing. The complex was visited by the Emperors of the Ming and Qing dynasties for annual ceremonies of prayer to Heaven for good harvest.

Location: Beijing, China

Built from: 1406 to 1420

Other places to visit nearby: Hongqiao Market, Tiananmen Square, The Palace Museum and many more.
