Cape Town

Also known as the Mother City, Cape town is port city on South Africa’s southwest coast,beneath the imposing Table Mountain with slowly rotating cable cars and Robben Island, a museum which back then held Nelson Mandela as a prisoner.

Location: Cape Town, South Africa

Best time to visit: March to May and from September to November. These shoulder seasons boast enviable weather, fewer crowds, and lower prices.

Places to visit in Cape Town: Clifton and Camps Bay Beaches, Table Mountain, Robben Island, The Victoria and Alfred Waterfront and many more.

Tanzania

An East African country known for its vast wilderness areas. They include the plains of Serengeti National Park, a safari mecca populated by the “big five” game (elephant, lion, leopard, buffalo, rhino), and Kilimanjaro National Park, home to Africa’s highest mountain. Offshore lie the tropical islands of Zanzibar, with Arabic influences, and Mafia, with a marine park home to whale sharks and coral reefs.

Location: Tanzania, East Africa

Accepted Currency Tanzanian shilling

Official Language Swahili, English

Best time to visit: Dry season, from late June to October,

Places to visit in Tanzania: Ngorongoro Conservation Area, Serengeti National Park, Arusha, Dar es Salaam, Stone Town,Zanzibar Archipelago and many more.

Pyramid of Giza

The Egyptian pyramids are ancient pyramid-shaped masonry structures located in Egypt.Most were built as tombs for the country's pharaohs and their consorts during the Old and Middle Kingdom periods.The pyramid of Giza is the largest and oldest of the 3 pyramid in the Giza Complex. It is the oldest of the Seven Wonders of the Ancient World, and the only one to remain largely intact.

Location: Giza Necropolis, Egypt

Constructed in: c. 2580–2560 BC (4th dynasty)

Best time to visit: Winter or early spring(October to April)

Other places to visit nearby: Great Sphinx The grand Egyptian Museum, Wissa Wassef Art Centre and many more.

Marrakesh

Marrakesh, a former imperial city in western Morocco, is a major economic center and home to mosques, palaces and gardens. The medina is a densely packed, walled medieval where thriving souks (marketplaces) sell traditional textiles, pottery and jewelry.

Location: Morroco

Accepted Currency: Moroccan dirham

Best time to visit: April-May

Other places to visit nearby: Jama El f'na Market, Jardin Majorelle, Bahia Palace and many more.

Johannesburg

Also called the city of gold, Johannesburg, South Africa's biggest city and capital of Gauteng province, began as a 19th-century gold-mining settlement. Its sprawling Soweto township was once home to Nelson Mandela and Desmond Tutu. Mandela’s former residence is now the Mandela House museum.

Location: South Africa

Best Time to Visit Year round destination

Other places to visit nearby: Apartheid Museum, Gold Reef City, Mandela's House, Hector Peiterson Memorial many more.

Réunion Island

Previously known as Bourbon, now Réunion Island, a French department in the Indian Ocean, is known for its volcanic, rainforested interior, coral reefs and beaches. Its most iconic landmark is Piton de la Fournaise, a climbable active volcano standing 2,632m (8,635 ft).

Location: Africa

Accepted Currency Euro

Best time to visit May and November

Other places to visit nearby: Piton de la Fournaise, Saint-Denis, Cilaos, Salazie and many more.
